## Overview
Set up a new documentation space for your project in a few steps.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your dashboard">
    Log in at `https://app.knowiterp.com` to access your dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start a project">
    Click the "New Project" button and fill in project details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Organize your docs">
    Add pages and sections as needed. Use drag-and-drop to reorder.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and share">
    Click "Save". Use the "Share" button to invite your team.
  </Step>
</Steps>
