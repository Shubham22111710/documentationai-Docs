## Quick Steps

1. Log in to your Knowiterp dashboard.
2. Click "New Project" on the top right.
3. Enter your project name and description.
4. Click "Create".
5. Start adding pages and organizing them into sections.

<Callout kind="tip">
You can invite team members by clicking "Share" in your project dashboard.
</Callout>
